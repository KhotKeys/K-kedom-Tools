Our Smart Farming Solutions

Comprehensive tools designed to address the specific challenges faced by refugee farmers

Soil & Weather Monitoring

Real-time monitoring of soil health, moisture levels, pH, and weather forecasts. Get crop suitability recommendations based on current conditions to make informed planting decisions.

Yield Prediction Tool

AI-powered crop yield predictions using environmental data. Plan for food needs and potential sales with confidence, reducing uncertainty and improving harvest planning.

Water Management

Smart water monitoring using satellite data to target irrigation only where needed. Conserve water resources while ensuring optimal crop growth and reducing waste.

Learning Platform

Access comprehensive farming guides, video tutorials, and best practices. Learn sustainable agriculture techniques, pest control, and soil improvement methods.

Resource Sharing

Community dashboard for tracking crop production and equitable distribution of tools, seeds, and resources. Ensure all farmers have access to essential equipment.

Seed Bank & Trading

Community seed bank management system for borrowing, trading, and accessing high-quality seeds. Improve crop productivity with better seed varieties.
